libNeST Documentation

libNeST stands for Library for Neutron Star Toolkit. It contains a variety of tools that help with handling the complicated physics of neutron stars. The aim is to enable users to work with and learn about the physics of nuclear matter inside a neutron star without worrying about the complexity of Skyrme functionals or data formats.
