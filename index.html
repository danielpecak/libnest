libNeST Documentation

libNeST stands for library for Neutron Star Toolkit. It contains a variety of tools that help handling in the complicated physics of neutrons stars. The aim is to use and learn about physics of nuclear matter inside a neutron star without thinking about complexity of Skyrme functionals or data format.

  • WBSK Toolkit
