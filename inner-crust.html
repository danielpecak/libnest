Bulk neutron properties

Quantities extracted from simulations for each density of the inner crust \(\bar\rho\): \(\rho_{Bn}\) – bulk density of neutrons, \(\Delta_n\) – pairing energy of neutrons, \(k_{\mathrm{F}}\) – wave vector calculated for bulk density of neutrons, \(\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}\) – Fermi energy, \(\epsilon_{\mathrm{F}}^*\) – Fermi energy calculated with regard to effective mass, \(N\) – number of neutrons, \(\xi\) – coherence length, \(R\) – radius of impurity, \(M_{\mathrm{eff}}\) – effective mass of impurity.
